University Men's Domitory Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ section, where we aim to provide quick and helpful answers to the questions you may have. Whether you're seeking information about our services, policies, or anything else, you'll find concise and informative responses here.

  • How do I apply for a spot in the men's dormitory?

    Typically, the university will provide application instructions on their housing website. It often involves filling out an online application form and submitting necessary documents.

  • What amenities are usually available in the men's dormitory?

    Men's dormitories typically offer a range of amenities, including shared bedrooms, communal bathrooms, common areas, study spaces, and sometimes recreational facilities like a gym or game room.

  • Can I choose my roommate?

    Some universities allow you to request a specific roommate, while others use a random assignment system. Check with your university's housing office for their specific policies.

  • Are cooking facilities available in the men's dormitory?

    Many dorms have communal kitchens equipped with basic appliances like microwaves and stoves. However, policies vary, and some dorms may only allow microwave use.

  • Is it possible to have guests in the dormitory?

    Generally, most dorms allow guests, but there are often rules and regulations to ensure the safety and comfort of all residents. Check with your dorm's rules and regulations for more.

  • What security measures are in place?

    Dormitories usually have security measures such as security personnel, and surveillance cameras to ensure the safety of residents.

  • Can I decorate my dorm room?

    Yes, you can usually personalize your space with decorations within the guidelines set by the university. Avoid causing damage to the walls or furniture.

  • Are there quiet hours in the dormitory?

    Many dorms have designated quiet hours to ensure a conducive environment for studying and rest. Check the dormitory rules for specific details.

  • How is laundry handled in the men's dormitory?

    Dorms typically have laundry facilities for boarders.

  • What should I bring to the men's dormitory?

    Besides personal items, check with the university for a recommended list. Essentials often include bedding, toiletries, kitchenware (if allowed), and any specific items based on dormitory rules.

University of Southern Mindanao Men's Dormitory Rule
Rule No. Rule Description
1 That every resident should bring necessary appliances.
2 That every resident should provide a time schedule to the dorm manager/attendant to be posted in one of the corners of the lobby for ready reference.
3 That resident should behave in a manner that be speaks of a dignified person worthy of respect by others.
4 That a resident is STRICTLY PROHIBITED from bringing visitors INSIDE HIS ROOM.
5 That visitors must ONLY BE ENTERTAINED at the RECEIVING ROOM.
6 That a resident must be in proper attire when entertaining visitors.
7 That observed CURFEW HOURS IS 9:00 P.M. Residents who will stay outside of the dormitory beyond curfew hour must ask permission from the dorm personnel and must sign the logbook.
8 That daily cleaners will be assigned and scheduled to clean the hallway, receiving hall, lobby, and kitchen.
9 That a resident should use the dormitory facilities properly and with utmost care.
10 That shouting, talking aloud, boisterous laughter, running in the corridors, or kicking doors, and the use of radio/cassettes are strictly prohibited, especially during rest/quiet hours at 12:30-2:00 PM and after 7:00 PM to 5:00 AM.
11 That parents are allowed to visit their son and can sleep for a night. Stays beyond one day will be charged Php. 100.00 per night.
12 That COOKING AND EATING SHOULD BE AT THE KITCHEN/DINING HALL ONLY. NON-RESIDENTS ARE NOT ALLOWED TO EAT HABITUALLY WITH THE RESIDENTS.
13 That a resident may be asked to do special work/assignment in preparation for any dormitory or school activities.
14 Smoking and drinking liquor are strictly prohibited inside the dormitory.
15 That residents are required to attend regular schedule/special meetings called upon by the dorm manager or authorized persons.
16 That dormitory rental is considered non-refundable if a resident is terminated due to house rule violations.
17 That residents who wish to join an Educational Field Trip or Off-Campus activities should secure permission from the dorm manager/attendant.
